Coffee House

A coffee shop is a casual establishment that specializes in serving coffee, tea, light refreshements. They offer a relaxed and inviting atmosphere for customers to socialize, work, or simply unwind. These establishments typically provide a variety of coffee drinks, such as espresso base beverages, drip coffee, and specialty options, alongside pastries, snacks, and sometimes light meals.

Popular Items

Americano

An Americano is a smooth, balanced coffee
made by adding hot water to espresso.
This process softens the strong espresso
flavor while preserving its rich aroma and
bold character. It's perfect for those who
enjoy the depth of espresso with the lighter
body of drip coffee.

Capuccino

Cappuccino is a classic Italian coffee drink
celebrated for perfect balance bold espresso
velvety steamed milk, and airy milk foam.
Traditionally served in a small cup, it features
parts of each component—creating a smooth
with a creamy texture
of coffee.

Mocha

Mocha coffee is a rich, indulgent beverage that
blends the bold flavor of espresso with the smooth
sweetness of chocolate. Traditionally, it’s made by
combining freshly brewed espresso, steamed milk,
and cocoa powder or chocolate syrup, then topped
with a layer of milk foamr whipped cream
of coffee.
